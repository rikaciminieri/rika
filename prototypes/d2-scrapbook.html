About Me
自己紹介

Half-Japanese full-stack engineer at Eventbrite. Former microbiology student turned tech advocate. I spend my days building features that help people find their next favorite experience — and my nights building tiny book nooks and ranking grilled cheeses.

I also rank grilled cheeses competitively (recruiters love this)

When I'm not coding, I'm probably doing pilates, watching Bridgerton for the third time, hustling at Monopoly Deal, or explaining why Michael Jackson is objectively the king of pop.

Hills I'll die on: The Rock's Jumanji is actually a good movie. Health and time with people you love is the most important thing in the world.

日本が大好き
Japan 日本
heritage, not decoration
東京 → 京都
新幹線 Shinkansen ¥13,320
One way 2h 15min
✈️ annual trip
5

I go to Japan almost every year. It's not a vacation — it's going home to the other half of who I am. Onsens at dawn, konbini runs at 2am, getting hopelessly lost in Shibuya and somehow ending up at the best ramen shop in the prefecture.

the konbini egg sandwich changed my life

My Japanese media diet: Terrace House (I have opinions), Yoasobi on repeat, Hana Yori Dango rewatch every winter, Dodo for when I need to feel things. I dream in both languages.

The onsen culture is something I think about constantly. The ritual of it — the quiet, the heat, the deliberate slowness. It's the opposite of tech culture and maybe that's why I need it.

日本語も話せます ✨

Luffy's true dream isn't just to be King of the Pirates — it's to throw the biggest party the world has ever seen. Where everyone, from every sea, is free to eat and laugh together. It connects to Joy Boy, the All Blue, and the destruction of the Red Line.
The Void Century
click to flip →
The Void Century was erased because the Ancient Kingdom had technology that could unite the world. The World Government destroyed it to maintain division and control. The poneglyphs are a message from that lost civilization — a recipe for freedom.
Joy Boy's Identity
click to flip →
Joy Boy wasn't a single person — it's a title passed down to whoever inherits the will of freedom. Luffy is the current Joy Boy. The straw hat is the symbol of this inheritance, passed from Joy Boy → Imu → Roger → Shanks → Luffy.
